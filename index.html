THE DEFENDERS

Inspired by Crypto Currency Candlesticks and Culture, we have created 10K Bulls and 10K Bears. Every Defender has its own traits, including skins, expressions and their favorite token.

THE RARITY

Besides their appearance, each Defender holds a Candle. Just as in real charts there are 5 Candle types, each more Bullish/Bearish than the previous one.

The Challenges

This is a series of collector's challenges, mini-games, and puzzles. Winners will have their Defender's Candle promoted to Marobuzu.

The Burned Ones

You Will have the option to burn Defenders to offer their soul to the Blockchain Gods and upgrade another defender of your choice.

The Metaverse

We will develop a universe for our Candle Defenders. Bulls and Bears Hodlers gain access to our Metaverse for the first time!

The Airdrop

The clan who has the less member for both Bulls and Bears will receive a huge surprise.

The Giving Back

10% of all primary sale proceeds will belong to our community to decide what to do with it. Share it between Hodlers? A Charity project? Your voice matters and we will let you choose.
